NetSentry Secure Gateway

NetSentry is a Debian-based security gateway. Client devices connect to the NetSentry Wi-Fi AP, receive controlled DHCP/DNS settings, and have their traffic routed through filtering, firewall policy, monitoring, IDS visibility, and logs before it reaches the upstream ISP network.
